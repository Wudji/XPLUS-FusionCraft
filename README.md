## XPlus FusionCraft: Where Innovation Meets Integration

XPlus FusionCraft is a 1.20.1 vanilla-style modpack based on Forge and the [Sinytra Connector](https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/sinytra-connector) module from Sinytra. It marks a groundbreaking achievement by enabling simultaneous loading of Fabric and Forge mods.
**Note: XPlus FusionCraft is currently in its experimental phase. Many features have not been thoroughly tested and could potentially lead to issues such as crashes or corrupted/inaccessible saves. Remember to backup your game data and save files before using it.**

### Uniting Vanilla with XPlus FusionCraft

XPlus FusionCraft retains a distinct 'vanilla' style, seamlessly integrating all elements introduced by XPlus FusionCraft with the original game. This harmony adds to the aesthetic enjoyment. Furthermore, XPlus FusionCraft does not heavily restrict or modify mod functionality, providing an authentic and immersive mod experience, enhancing the joy of exploration.

### Exemplary Performance, Crafted for Excellence

Leveraging the optimization experience from the XPlus series' core integrations, XPlus FusionCraft offers exceptional performance, ensuring a smooth and dynamic world, delivering the most fluid gaming experience.

### Boundless Exploration, Bridging Worlds

With the support of Sinytra Connector, XPlus FusionCraft achieves the simultaneous loading of Fabric and Forge mods, exploring a path to resolve the longstanding choice between loaders and mods. This advancement introduces a richer and more unique experience, transcending boundaries and offering an infinite world of possibilities.
